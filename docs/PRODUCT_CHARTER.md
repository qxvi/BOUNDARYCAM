# BOUNDARYCAM Product Charter

BOUNDARYCAM is the camera for machine action.

Its job is to make boundary crossing visible before interpretation, trust, or compliance language begins.

Primary question: What crossed the boundary?

BOUNDARYCAM is a public product/control surface. It shows the shape of a machine-action boundary frame.

BOUNDARYCAM does not claim to be the final evidence authority.

BOUNDARYCAM does not claim legal proof.

BOUNDARYCAM does not claim runtime enforcement unless connected to an enforcement runtime.

BOUNDARYCAM makes the boundary inspectable.
