Not AI trust. Boundary visibility.

BOUNDARYCAM exists because machine actions are becoming world-touching events. The output is not enough. The boundary must be captured.

Before you trust the output, capture the boundary.

BOUNDARYCAM makes machine action legible without pretending that a visual surface is the source of truth. It exposes the question, the frame, and the condition. Evidence, verification, and closure remain bounded to the proper systems.
