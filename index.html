BOUNDARYCAM

The camera for machine action.

Before you trust the output, capture the boundary.

Software no longer only produces text. It calls tools, submits forms, sends messages, modifies files, approves records, deletes objects, publishes artifacts, and changes state. When that happens, the important question is what crossed the boundary.

If it touched the world, it needs a boundary frame.

BOUNDARYCAM does not label systems as trusted or untrusted. It shows whether authority, execution, evidence, replay, recognition, recourse, and closure are present.
